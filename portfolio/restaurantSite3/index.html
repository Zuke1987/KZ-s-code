Restaurant Description
This restaurant was founded in 2018 by cousins Brian and Kevin Zuhoski with the hopes of sharing classic American cuisine with the residents of Long Island, NY. Our restaurant features a full bar with some of the nation's highest ranked ales and lagers and a variety of beers from Long Island's local breweries. Our menu offers American classics such as hot dogs and burgers, Texas style Brisket, and several other dishes which will satisfy the palette of any diner.
Along with exquisite cuisine another highlight of our restaurant are our wall to wall flat screen TV's which are always showing the latest and most exciting sporting events. Come on down and enjoy some mouth-watering food, premium beer, and the sports arena like ambience of our restaurant.
